Suggested pre-work:

Although you don't need any prior skills or experience to do well in this course, we recommend you are at least familiar with HTML and CSS (no need to be an expert though!).

Either attend a Web Design Beginner weekend workshop, or find a free introduction course online (like Codecademy).
